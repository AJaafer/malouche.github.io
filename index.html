I’m Dhafer Malouche, a Professor of Statistics at the University of Carthage. I teach at the Ecole Supérieure de la Statistique et de l’Analyse de l’Information several data science courses: Big Data, Computing tools for Statistics such as R and Python, Bayesian Statistics and Time Series.

I’m especially interested in machine learning, statistical modelling and data visualization. I am using every day R, Python, H2O and Spark for my teaching and research. I learn a lot from other people’s code. It gave me ideas for my own work and I like sharing my codes with others. Your feedback, comments and questions are then very welcome.

You will find in this website several tutorials about R, Python, Big Data, Machine Learning, all my published papers and you can learn more about my research interests.

Links and tutorials on my R packages are also available in this website. This page is still under construction and I’m working hard to make it available soon. I will posting R packages when you can perform PCA, MCA Correlation Analysis with a simple Shiny App.

So feel free to contact me
